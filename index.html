Digital Legacy

Family safety
Purpose: If something happens to you, your family can recover what matters (IDs, policies, contacts).
Important: LifeVault can’t “auto-send” anything without a backend. This feature helps you set a clean, simple plan.
Do NOT store your Master Password in plaintext.
Best practice: write it on paper, seal it, store it in a safe / deposit box, and reference the location above.
